Cheyenne grew up in the middle of a transitioning world. At the turn of the century, technology was rapidly growing, yet dial up was still dominant and "smart phones" were not a thought. At the age of 9, she took a part in disassembling an old Dell desktop, and was intrigued with all of its workings.

In college, once she took her first MIS class she knew that she had found her major. Programming, database creation, and cyber security were the classes she loved the most. They were all challenging, yet rewarding.

From the months of August to December in 2016, she studied abroad in Arezzo, Italy and earned her International Area Studies minor while exploring Europe.
